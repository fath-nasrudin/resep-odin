Nasi Goreng

Nasi Goreng is a popular Indonesian fried rice dish that's beloved throughout Southeast Asia. This flavorful dish combines tender rice with a mix of vegetables, eggs, and savory seasonings, all stir-fried to perfection. The combination of soy sauce, garlic, and aromatic spices creates a delicious meal that can be enjoyed for breakfast, lunch, or dinner.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (peas, corn, etc.)
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Green onions for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  2. Add the garlic and onion, and saut√© until fragrant.
  3. Add the carrot and mixed vegetables, and cook until tender.
  4. Push the vegetables to one side of the pan and pour in the beaten eggs. Scramble them until cooked.
  5. Add the cooked rice and soy sauce, mixing everything together. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Cook for another 2-3 minutes, stirring frequently.
  7. Garnish with green onions before serving.
